I'm Yiferu Mekonnen, a dedicated Data Science student at Debre Berhan University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree. My academic journey has fueled a deep passion for transforming complex datasets into actionable insights and intelligent AI systems.

I specialize in the full data science pipeline — from exploratory analysis and feature engineering through to deploying end-to-end machine learning and deep learning solutions. Beyond technical work, I'm driven by real-world impact: detecting hate speech to build safer online communities, forecasting agricultural and business outcomes, and leveraging remote sensing to address environmental challenges in Ethiopia's highlands.
